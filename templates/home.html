Brain MRI Tumor Classification

Upload a brain MRI image to generate a fast model prediction for one of four classes: no tumor, glioma, meningioma, or pituitary.

Clinical Context

A brain tumor is an abnormal growth of cells in brain tissue. This tool assists with preliminary image screening and is intended to support, not replace, expert clinical evaluation.

  • Glioma: originates from glial cells in the brain or spine.
  • Meningioma: develops in the meninges surrounding the brain and spinal cord.
  • Pituitary: forms in the pituitary gland and can affect endocrine function.
  • No Tumor: MRI appearance not classified as tumorous by the model.
